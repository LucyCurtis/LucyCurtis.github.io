We,                the undersigned, are
                               graphic designers,
art directors and
visual communicators

who have been raised in a
world in which the techniques and apparatus of advertising have persistently been presented to us as the most
lucrative, effective and desirable use of our talents. Many design teachers and mentors promote this belief;
the market rewards it; a tide of books and publications reinforces it.
